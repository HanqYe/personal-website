About — Qiye Han

Qiye Han

Economics · Renmin University of China · Exchange at UChicago

I am a third-year undergraduate in Economics at Renmin University of China, currently on a one-year exchange at the University of Chicago. My research interests center on how technological innovation shapes investment — from platform design and investor behavior to NLP-enabled measurement in financial research.

I have research experience working with Professor Celine Yue Fei on ESG investment and platform nudges, and with Professor Ziteng Lei on representation learning and embeddings. I am ranked in the top 5% of my cohort and aspire to pursue a PhD in Finance or Economics.

Research Interests

  • ▶ Platform Design & Investor Behavior — ESG investment, behavioral nudges
  • ▶ NLP & Computational Finance — embeddings, LLMs, asset pricing
  • ▶ Behavioral & Experimental Economics — RCTs, DiD, causal forests
  • ▶ Economic Theory & Modeling — dynamic equilibrium, fintech
